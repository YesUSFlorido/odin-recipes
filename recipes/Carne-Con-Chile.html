Carne Con Chile

Ahh! the good old and easy Carne Con Chile! its a staple in Sonora's cuisine. The coolest part? it's easy to make! The best part? it's delicious!

Ingredients:

Carne Con Chile its really simple you just need four things:
  • Chile colorado sauce
    • If you want to learn how to make it here its a link!
  • cooked and Shredded beef
  • Salt
  • Beef juice
  • fat or oil
  • water

Instructions:

  1. first you need to put on a pot oil or fat
  2. you just need to pour the chile colorado sauce in the pot, be careful that the oil its not that hot
  3. then you put the shredded meat in the pot
  4. then mix!
  5. if the meat looks too thick add a bit more water
  6. enjoy!
