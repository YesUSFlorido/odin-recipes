Carne Con Chile

Ahh! the good old and easy Carne Con Chile! its a staple in Sonora's cuisine. The coolest part? it's easy to make! The best part? it's delicious!

Ingredients:

Carne Con Chile its really simple you just need five things:
  • Chile colorado Sauce
  • Shredded meat
    • Beef
    • Pork
    • Chicken
  • Salt
  • consommé
  • fat or oil
  • water

If you are vegan omit the meat and add some veggies!

Instructions:

  1. first you need to put on a pot oil or fat
  2. you just need to pour the chile colorado sauce in the pot, the oil is going to be hot so be careful
  3. then you put the shredded meat in the pot
  4. then mix!
  5. if the meat looks too thick add a bit more water
  6. enjoy!
