Pozole

Pozole its kinda like a universal thing in mexico; every state has its own type of pozole and Sonora its not exception. but it doesn't matter what type of pozole is; pozole its pozole and everyone loves it!

In the state of Sonora, pozole its a light spicy soup with pork and nixtamal. Its usually served with corn chips and chopped vegetables like cucumber, lettuce and radishes. And although its commonly served with pork and nixtamal, there are many variants that changes the ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Pork (Specifically chamorro)
  • Nixtamal
  • Chile Colorado
  • Achiote
  • Spices
  • Salt
