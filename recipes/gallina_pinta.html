Gallina pinta its odd. First its named literally translates to “Painted Chicken” even though its a beef soup. Secondly, nobody really knows why its called like that…

theres a story that says some cowboys got together and made a soup out of the things they had (this being: beans, maize and beef); after putting the ingredients in the pot a chicken started jumping around it, making the cowboys yell: “¡Aguas con la Gallina Pinta!”; making everyone believe that was the name of the soup.

Other theory suggest that perhaps the name comes from the color that the beans and maize make on the soup, however that doesn’t explain where the chicken comes from…
